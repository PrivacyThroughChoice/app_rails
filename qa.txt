General
-------

1) Can log in
2) Can log out
3) Cannot register

Guest
-----

1) Can search moderated as guest
2) Can view moderated as guest
3) Cannot view unmoderated as guest

User
----

1) Can create service
2) Can edit own unmoderated service
3) Cannot edit own moderated service
4) Cannot edit other user's service
